What is WeResponse?

Every year, it appears that the world becomes more dangerous and disaster-prone. Stories of strong storms and deadly earthquakes seem to hug the headlines these days.This further underscores the need for disaster preparedness. But when in looking for a way to reach people during an emergency situation, what is the best, most effective method? Is it through messages? Is push notifications enough? Should you still use the traditional phone call? Or is it a combination of all methods? WeResponse made it possible to put all these methods in one. WeResponse aims to become the most reliable medium of emergency alerts, if you contact WeResponse, it will call all the possible emergency aid you needed. WeResponse have all the emergency aid that people usually call, there is no need to call every emergency aid one by one, WeResponse will call every emergency aid you needed for faster response to the emergency situation.
